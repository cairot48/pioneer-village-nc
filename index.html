About Pioneer Village

Tucked into the Heart of Maggie Valley

Nestled along Campbell Creek in the Great Smoky Mountains, Pioneer Village is a 14-cabin resort built for people who want to actually get away. No cookie-cutter lodging. No crowds. Just mountain air, creek sounds, and a porch with a rocking chair.

Under new ownership, we've updated the experience while keeping everything that makes this place special: the stone fireplaces, the full kitchens, the creek views, and the quiet.
